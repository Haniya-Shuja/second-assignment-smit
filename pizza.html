PIZZA

Pizza is one of my favourite junk food

Ingredients

  1. Yougurt: 1cup
  2. Baking powder: 1 tea spoon
  3. All purpose floor: 1 cup
  4. water: as per required to form thick dough
  5. Sugar: 1teaspoon
  6. chicken: 50 grm
  7. Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
  8. Salt : teaspoon
  9. Turmeric: 1/2teaspoon
  10. Chat masala: 1 teaspoon
  11. Chili sauce: To taste
  12. soya sauce: To taste
  13. Ginger Garlic paste: 1 teaspoon
  14. Cheese: 1cup
  15. vegetables: 1 cup
  16. vineger: 1 teaspoon

Method

  • Take a bowl and add one cup of yougrt and all purpose floor into bowl.
  • Add one tea spoon of sugar and baking powder,pinch of salt and mix it well.
  • After that graually add water and stirr it into thick better
  • Take another bowl add chicken,ginger garlic paste, soya sauce, chili sauce,chat masala,salt,turmeric,red chili powder,vineger and cover it with the sheet for half an hour.
  • Take out the tawa or pan put into the stoves pre heat for five minutes.
  • Now cook the chicken marrination into another pan with low flame untill the colour change into golden brown.
  • when the tawa become preheated grease the pan with oil then pour and spread the better or mixture of dough into the pan.
  • Cover it with th lid.
  • cook on the lowest heat for fifteen minutes after flip into another side and turning off the flame.
  • Spread pizza sauce, Cheese,add vegetables, marrinated chicken,pinch of oregano,one tea spoon of chili flakes and garnish it with again cheese into the pizza bread.
  • turn on the flame and cover it for again 15 to 20 minutes.
  • When cheese melt and pizza colour change then take out into the srving dish.
  • Serve it with the tomato ketchup and chli garlic sauce.
